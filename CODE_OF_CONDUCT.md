# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical facts and reproducible details.
- Accept feedback and discuss disagreements professionally.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.
